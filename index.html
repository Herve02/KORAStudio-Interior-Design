The Studio

WHO
WE ARE and what we believe

KORA Studio was founded in Kigali in 2015 with a single conviction: that the built environment should provoke feeling, not just function. We are a team of seven architects, designers, and craftspeople united by a refusal to settle for the expected.

Our work spans private residences, corporate headquarters, hotels, and cultural institutions across Rwanda and East Africa. Each project is a new argument — for beauty, for boldness, for spaces that make people feel something they cannot quite name.

Service 04

SPATIAL
ARCHITECTURE

Sometimes a great interior is not possible until the building itself is reimagined. We do both.

Our spatial architecture service addresses the bones of a building — floor plan reconfigurations, wall removals, volume changes, new openings, and structural interventions — to unlock the full potential of a space before interior design begins. We work with our network of structural engineers and contractors to deliver the changes safely and beautifully.

What Is Included

  • Structural and spatial audit with as-built survey
  • Floor plan reconfiguration options
  • Coordination with structural engineers
  • Volume and opening modification design
  • Natural light optimisation strategy
  • Construction drawings and specifications
  • Contractor briefing and site supervision

How We Work

THE
PROCESS

01

Phase One

DISCOVER

We spend time inside the space and inside your thinking. We observe light, measure flows, and listen to everything you want and everything you cannot articulate. This phase ends when we know the project better than you do.

02

Phase Two

CONCEIVE

We develop a bold concept — one clear idea that drives every decision. Materials, volumes, light, furniture, and texture are brought together into a presentation that makes your vision visible for the first time.

03

Phase Three

SPECIFY

Every detail is documented. Technical drawings, 3D renders, material schedules, lighting diagrams. Nothing reaches a craftsperson or contractor until it has been drawn and approved with absolute precision.

04

Phase Four

DELIVER

We manage every installation, coordinate every trade, and stay on-site until the last detail is right. We hand over not a project, but a transformation — and we do not leave until you feel it.
